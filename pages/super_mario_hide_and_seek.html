Super Mario Hide & Seek
Published by UnspeakableGaming, MooseCraft, EpicStun, AwesomeElina, SSundee, & Pixel Gamer in 2020 Watch Now

Created for PopularMMOs distributed by RedSmileyFace.

This map was created on 1.15.5 using Vanilla Minecraft! All furniture, models, and textures were provided by RedSmileyFace. All builds were created by RedSmileyFace.

A Hide and Seek map dedicated and tailored to Unspeakable Gaming, MooseCraft, and 09SharkBoy as they influence many of us! It is a all new Hide and Seek Map with Custom Morph Outfits, Custom Items, and much more... Fully Mario themed including outfits such as Peach, Mario, and Luigi. Beginning your Adventure as you put on the Resource Pack with all the Custom Models.

Installation Guide

The following steps will guide you through how to download and install this map as well as getting it launched as you join it for an epic adventure!

  • Purchase this map for FREE by clicking Here!
  • Download the .zip File provided after the Purchase.
  • Locate to C:/Users/[CURRENT USER]/Downloads/ and extract the newly downloaded .zip File.
  • Open your Minecraft Launcher
  • Choose 1.14.4 Minecraft (Vanilla) from the Installations Section of the Launcher
  • Place the Extracted Folder in your %appdata%/.minecraft/saves/ folder!
  • Under JVM Arguments change -Xmx2G to -Xmx[Number of GB]G which will allow you to dedicate more Memory / Ram to Minecraft! (Allowing it to run smoother with modiciations.)
