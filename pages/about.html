The Journey
Changing the game.

Over 4 years of building experience, Red has taken on positions ranging from server development to map and build creation for some of the most aspiring Minecraft content creators. Red has dedicated himself to creating one-of-a-kind content to be played and enjoyed by members of the community with the end goal being to put a smile on your face.

He can be found creating maps for various influencers and hosting community-driven events to spur engagement and audience participation!
