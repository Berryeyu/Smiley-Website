Summoning the Smile Room

by Smiley
27th May, 2021
Summoning the Smile Room!
Published by MooseCraft on Dec 6, 2020 Watch Now

Created for Moosecraft distributed by 100 Media HQ.

This map was created on 1.12.2 using the MooseMedia HQ Mod-Pack! All furniture, models, and textures were provided by MooseMedia HQ. All builds were created by Smiley.

Have you ever wanted to summon the Smile Room?! Well now you can! Join a Friend and Launch into this epic Custom Modded Map as you work your way through the adventure in the Boaling Alley to gather parts to summon the Smile Room!

As you enter the smile room through the bowling alley you will work your way through complex obstacles such as: mazes, parkour, and room escapes! You will be faced with challenges such as collecting key cards and finding your way out of "impossible" scenarios.
